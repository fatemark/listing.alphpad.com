# ABOUT SWAPA
---
SWAPA is the first veDEX on alephium

---
## Mission
Swapa aims to create a veDEX with deep liquidity. Our liquidity providers can expect high returns. All emissions will go to the liquidity providers and all fees go to voters. 
---
## Vision
Our vision is to bring value to the Alephium ecosystem, making it a place to create deep liquidity with high yields.

---
## Values
- **Decentralization**:Voters and users who hold a stake in the platform should be able to decide where emissions go, not a centralized team making these decisions
- **Fairness**:Emissions and fees should be distributed proportionally according to respectively the liquidity provided and SWAPA locked into xSWAPA
- **Farming**:Bigger APRs are better APRs
